# To Aramadanda (AN 2:36)

## Causation Model(s)

### Quote(s):
> * It's with relishing, possession, greed, bondage, & entrenchment in passion for sensuality as a cause 
>   * that noble warriors dispute with noble warriors, brahmans dispute with brahmans, and householders dispute with householders.
> * it's with relishing, possession, greed, bondage, & entrenchment in passion for views as a cause 
>   * that contemplatives dispute with contemplatives
