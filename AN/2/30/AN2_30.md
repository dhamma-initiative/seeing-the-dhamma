# Release (AN 2:30)

## Causation Model(s)

### Quote(s):
> * Defiled by passion, 
>   * the mind is not released. 
> * Defiled by ignorance, 
>   * discernment does not develop.
>
> * From the fading of passion 
>   * is there awareness-release. 
> * From the fading of ignorance 
>   * is there discernment-release
